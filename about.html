About myself

All my life I've been more on the creative side, whether it was drawing or making different things. I always wanted to be able to build but I never learned any specific craft. Along with the interest to make or build, I’ve always had a natural inclination when it comes to learning languages. I love breaking down the different pieces of the syntax and semantics. These two pieces of my life have really shaped where I am today.


Languages

While development and programming languages aren’t exactly the same as human/word based languages, there’s still a strong similarity when it comes to what semantics and syntax mean. In coding, syntax is talked about constantly, so the arrangement and everything being in the proper order to work. Just like the order of a sentence. You also need to understand the semantics, which is what the word means or in coding what the different pieces or elements do.


Teamwork

I'm very outgoing and love working with people. I can buckle down and work on a project by myself, but I draw energy from interacting with those around me and being around others. I have a natural ability to take charge however, I truly love working on a team. Being in a support role is just as enjoyable to me as being the lead on a project. I also have a strong skill set when it comes to training others. I've excelled at this for years and pride myself on my ability to help and equip others.
